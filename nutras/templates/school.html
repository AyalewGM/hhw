​Professional Colon Hydrotherapy Training


PLEASE NOTE: Effective April 1st, 2021,
Hamri’s Health & Wellness, School of Colon Hydrotherapy
will be teaching using the new I-ACT PROFESSIONAL TRAINING CURRICULUM.


Prerequisites for Entry into Training and Certification

  • • Each student MUST have a high school diploma or equivalent requirement showing they have achieved high school standards.This may be documented by diploma, GED or equivalency exam such as the Wunderlich Ability to Benefit Test.
    ***This must be completed prior to entering into training
  • • Recommendation – each new student should have received 1 – 3 colonics prior to entering training.
  • • Must have a current CPR card (before certification)
  • • Each student MUST be an I-ACT member prior to taking any I-ACT training. The Instructor / School must collect current I-ACT administration fee ($1000.00 payable to I-ACT) which covers:
    1. a. I-ACT Membership Application
    2. b. NBCHT Membership Application including test fee
    3. c. Cengage registration fees
    This I-ACT administration fee MUST be received by the I-ACT Office prior to the start of any training either through Cengage, or training in-person at the training facility. This fee is non-refundable.
  • • Student MUST complete 265 hours of training and pass the National Board Exam in order to be certified and earn the title: I-ACT Certified Professional Colon Hydrotherapist

    • I-ACT’s Professional Colon Hydrotherapy Training is a 265-hour program


  • 1. 200 hours of online course modules through I-ACT/Cengage, covering the topics of Anatomy & Physiology, Microbiology, Intestinal Health, Nutrition, Drug Interactions, Business Ethics/Office Procedures and Complementary Modalities.
  • 2. 65 hours training with Hamri for the hands on training at the center including clients sessions.
  • 3. Total Certifications upon completion: during this course, you will earn up to eight certifications.


​Cost for the Professional Colon Hydrotherapy Training


Total tuition cost is: $6000.00


$1000.00 is Admin/Registration fee payable to I ACT and must be received with your application. (Non Refundable)


Remaining balance of 5000.00 is due prior to the start of your training.
